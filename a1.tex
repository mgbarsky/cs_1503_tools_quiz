\section{Sample variance}
Consider the same dataset:
${2, 3, 4, 5, 5, 6, 8}$. 

What is its variance?

Answer: The variance is $x=\frac{\sum_{i=0}^{n-1}{(x_i - \bar x)^2}}{n-1} = value $.
